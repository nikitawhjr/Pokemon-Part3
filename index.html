Pokemon Facts which you probably didn't know.

1. "Pika" is the Japanese word for the noise of electricity crackling and "chu" is how the Japanese describe the sound a mouse makes. That's how the name "Pikachu" was created.
2. Ash's Pikachu is first non-evolved Pokemon who has defeated Mega evolved Pokemons.
3. Charlizard doesn't listen to Ash, but when he does it is only when he is fighting with another FIRE Pokemon.
4. When Meowth started learning to read and talk, the first word he learnt was ROCKET.
5. Hitmonchan And Hitmonlee Got Their Names From Famous Fighters - Jackie Chan and Bruce Lee.
6. Magneton Weighs A Lot More Than Three Magnemites. Magneton weighs over 10 times the weight of a single Magnemite.
7. Tangela Originally Couldn't Use Vine Whip.
8. Ditto Is Most Likely A Failed Attempt At Cloning Mew.
9. Clefairy Was Almost The Face Of Pokemon. (The fairy Pokemon Clefairy was originally set to be Ash's starter Pokemon in the anime, instead of Pikachu. Due to a last minute change, Pikachu got the role instead, because of the creature's popularity. )
10. Each Spinda Has A Unique Pattern Of Spots.
11. Psychic-Type Weaknesses Are Based On Common Fears. Although Psychic-Types were hugely overpowered in the first generation of Pokemon, they do in fact have weaknesses in the form of Ghost, Dark, and Bug-types.
12. Mewtwo Comes Before Mew In The Pokedex.
13. Koffing And Weezing Were Going To Be Named After American Cities.
14. Professor Oak's Nidorino Has The Cry Of A Nidorina.
15. Zubat does not have eyes.
